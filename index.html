Welcome to School

Where every thing has a purpose

The School is promoted by Sajawal Siksha Sansthan Society, working towards education excellence and meets the need of highly demanding professionals since 2020, in the pursuit of serving the society through imparting education. The best faculty in the respective fields from reputed universities and other leading institutes is engaged to impart quality education.

R M Public School is a CBSE affiliated school & it is very proud moment for us to say that we got our affiliation on first month (January) of 2020 & our affiliation no. 330874 from CBSE. We can proudly say that we have worked very hard to achieve this, which has added shining stars to our school.

With a vision to impart education to every child, our teaching is based on the principle that every child is unique and gifted with exceptional abilities, thereby allowing them the freedom to discover their inherent potentials.
